Drawing
Together
  • Play with 2 or more players.
  • Each player has a different colored marker.
  • The players take clockwise turns
  1. First player: draw a small plus "+" sign anywhere on the paper.
  2. Next player: using your marker draw a straight line that extends from a line of the recent plus "+" sign. Continue the trajectory until you reach the edge of the paper, then draw a line at a relative 90 degrees and stop when you reach the edge of the paper.
  3. Finally, draw a small plus "+" sign anywhere on the lines you have just created.
  4. Each player take turns repeating steps 2-3.
